1. How to do a fund withdrawal from {{websiteData.WebTitle}} account?

Fund withdrawal can be transferred to your account via debit card or bank transfer.

2. Any requirement for bank account that I use for withdrawal transaction?

It is important to note that the payee name entered for withdrawal must be same with account name listed in {{websiteData.WebTitle}}. Please note that {{websiteData.WebTitle}} has a right to refuse any kind of transaction conducted by a third party name and withdrawal request that do not meet the stated policy will be rejected promptly.

3. What currencies are acceptable by {{websiteData.WebTitle}}?

{{websiteData.WebTitle}} currently only accept {{websiteData.CurrenyCode}} -> {{websiteData.CurrenyName}} for transactions.

4. How long does it take to process the withdrawal transaction?

Withdrawal transaction will be processed within 5 (five) minutes, however processing may take longer over the weekends/bank offline/bank holidays or if the account verification is needed for some cases.

4. How to withdraw?
  1. Please login to {{websiteData.WebTitle}} website and select "Transaction > Withdraw" on menu bar.
  2. Fill the withdraw amount without any dot/comma symbol. Ex: 1000 (one thousand ringgit).
  3. Choose the withdrawal bank type and select the bank option.
  4. Withdraw form must be filled using valid/correct data (full name, bank account no, bank name, etc).
  5. Select "Submit" and please be waited while your withdrawal is being processed.

Please note that any withdrawal transaction (in/out of promotion) should be adjusted to predetermined policies. {{websiteData.WebTitle}} has a right to refuse any kind of transaction if it does not comply with the policy. For further information please contact our customer service representative (online 24 hours).
